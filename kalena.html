Kalena Larka Teppenso

Kalena Larka Teppenso is a 19 year old female Lakirin. Her eyes are a light blue with black eyeliner. Her hair is not normal and is divided into slick, wet strips so it resembles elastic crystals. She loves the water and enjoys keeping her hair perpetually wet. Her hair is a light blond with light purple at the tips and strewn throughout. It is most often kept in a tight bun with a single strand or two hanging down in front of her ears and face. Her skin is a light opaque purple with dark blue jagged stripes going out from her eyes. She has an angular body with blue adorning all the tips. She likes to wear long dresses that communicate she is in control, but are also effective and easy to move in. Her power is water, and as such her weapons would conduct water
